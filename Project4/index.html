About Jon Arbuckle



Hi there Everyone! My name is Jon Arbuckle. I am 30 years old and I am a cartoonist that lives in Muncie, Indiana. I am owner of a cat named Garfield and a dog named Odie. Garfield is fat, lazy and loves to eat. While Odie is a dog without a brain.

Although my pets drive me crazy, I can´t imagine life without them.


I spend half of my time going to the grocery store to buy enough food to feed Garfield. I have to make sure I have bought Garfield´s favorite food, or else he´ll get grumpy. This includes milk, cheese, bread, ham, chicken wings, potato chips, pasta with marinara sause, frozen pizzas, and lots and lots of lasagña. When I come home, Garfield usually atacks me a tries to eat everything. I wasn´t too happy about that.
